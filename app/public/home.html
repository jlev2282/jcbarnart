BIOGRAPHY


b. 1949, Valdosta, GA

Live and work in Atlanta, GA

Jeannette Barnett was born Jeannette Carter in Valdosta, Georgia in 1949. A preacher's daughter and eldest daughter of 5 she would tell me that there was no time for sports and leisure life. T here was time for school and church. Keeping in this line with this disciplined and focused upbringing, Jeannette went on to college and became an accountant. She even worked for Price Waterhouse in New York, City before finally settling one Atlanta and the Georgia Tech budget office as her career pursuit. She served there 30 years and was one of the most senior employees in her department at the time of retirement 30 years later. And now in her retirement, there is time for leisure. To pursue a dream put off as a child, to become an artist. Jeannette explores multiple types of media in her art, determined to not be constrained again. Vibrant colors, vivid scenes, and deep topics are just a few of the themes and approaches you'll find in her work.
